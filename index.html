Frequently Asked Questions

We offer a comprehensive range of life insurance products including Term Life Insurance, Whole Life Insurance, Child Education Plans, Unit Linked Insurance Plans (ULIPs), Pension Plans, and various riders for critical illness and accident benefits. Our advisors can help you choose the right combination based on your needs and financial goals.

The ideal coverage amount depends on various factors including your income, debts, lifestyle, future goals, and family needs. A general rule is to have coverage equal to 10-15 times your annual income. However, we recommend a personalized assessment. Our experts will analyze your specific situation to recommend adequate coverage.

Our claim process is simple and hassle-free: 1) Intimate us about the claim via phone, email, or app, 2) Submit required documents (death certificate, policy document, claim form), 3) Our team verifies and processes the claim, 4) Settlement amount is disbursed to nominees. We aim to settle claims within 7-10 working days of receiving all documents.

Yes! We offer flexible premium payment modes including monthly, quarterly, half-yearly, and annual options. You can choose the frequency that suits your cash flow. Monthly payments can be made easily through auto-debit ECS, UPI, or online payments via our portal or mobile app.

Yes, life insurance policies offer attractive tax benefits. Premiums paid are eligible for deduction under Section 80C up to ₹1.5 lakh. The maturity proceeds and death benefits are tax-exempt under Section 10(10D). We recommend consulting with a tax advisor for personalized advice based on current tax laws.

We offer a grace period of 15-30 days depending on your payment mode. If you miss a payment, the policy remains in force during this period. For longer delays, policies can be revived within 2-5 years with payment of overdue premiums and interest. We send multiple reminders via SMS, email, and calls to help you avoid lapses.
